Hi, I'm Marta, and I passion about skin! I’m a wife, a skincare addict, a lash lift educator, a medical esthetician, and the founder of Skin & Touch. My passion is performing unforgettable facials that layer multiple techniques and technologies for the most spectacular results and my clients love it.

I created a beautiful studio setting for my clients, and my aim with every booking is to ensure they always leave feeling confident, with their skin glowing and their minds feeling relaxed. I value my time with clients, so I only do a maximum of 3-4 bookings per day to allow time to consult, treat and chat without the pressure of ever feeling rushed.

Also, I believe in taking a holistic approach when treating the skin. My medical degree allows me to better understand the underlying processes and reactions in the body, and how they impact the health and appearance of the skin. This knowledge allows me to provide personalized and effective skincare treatments that address not only surface-level concerns but also underlying issues."

I'm not ashamed to admit my love for skincare. My vanity has more products on it than clothing, shoes, and handbags in my closet combined. That’s because I'm always on the hunt to curate the best skincare in the industry to offer you. Skincare is fundamental to skin health, and my mission is to ensure you’re getting the best selections that provide results.
